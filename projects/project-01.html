Project 01

Handheld Device Concept

A compact interactive hardware concept combining enclosure design, electronics packaging, and embedded interface thinking.

Problem / Objective

Make a small device feel deliberate rather than cramped.

The concept explores how a dense portable object can balance tactile controls, internal board space, and visual clarity without becoming visually noisy or uncomfortable to hold.

Process

Layout, grip studies, then electronics packaging.

Early sketches established control zones and proportions. That was followed by enclosure volume tests, screen placement studies, and a simple internal architecture that kept assembly readable.
